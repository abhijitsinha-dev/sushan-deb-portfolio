About Sushan Deb

Passionate and innovative visual artist with a Bachelor of Visual Arts (Honours, First Division) from Government College of Art & Craft, Lichubagan, Agartala.

Specialising in traditional and contemporary painting techniques, I am dedicated to nurturing artistic talent by fostering creativity, technical proficiency, and self-expression. I strive to create inclusive, inspiring learning environments that adapt to diverse learning styles.

My expertise spans oil, acrylic, watercolor, and mixed media techniques, alongside a strong proficiency in traditional Indian folk art. I combine hands-on artistic expertise with digital proficiency to integrate technology into art education.

Core Skills

  • Oil, Acrylic, Watercolor & Mixed Media
  • Traditional Indian Folk Art
  • Portraiture & Figure Drawing
  • Adobe Photoshop & Corel Painter
  • Workshop Facilitation
